Food App

This design emphasizes user-friendliness with modern typography, vibrant color schemes, and consistent iconography for a seamless food-ordering experience.

Key Features: Home Screen: Displays featured restaurants, popular dishes, and personalized recommendations. Search & Filters: Users can easily search for food by cuisine, restaurant, or dish, with advanced filters for dietary preferences. Restaurant & Menu Pages: Clean layouts for browsing restaurant details, menus, reviews, and ratings. Order Tracking: A real-time progress tracker for deliveries. Checkout & Payment: Simple, secure payment options with a summary of the order. Profile & Order History: A user profile section with past orders, saved favorites, and settings. .
